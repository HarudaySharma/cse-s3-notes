<u>*Responsible for :*</u>
- **Process-to-process** *delivery* of a *packet*, part of a message, from *one process* to *another*. 
- Two *processes communicate* in a **client/server** *relationship*.

>[!tip]  
>- <u>**data link layer**</u> is <u>*responsible*</u> for *delivery* of *frames* between *two neighboring nodes* over a link.
>	- This is called **node-to-node** delivery.
>- The <u>**network layer**</u> is <u>*responsible*</u> for *delivery* of *datagrams* between *two hosts*.
>	- This is called **host-to-host** delivery.
